About

Hi, my name is Ikraam. I am a freelance consultant in the technology field.

I've worn many hats in my career in the spaces of education and technology. Although I am a proud jack of all trades, my experiences have led me to dive into software development, where I believe I can make the most impact.


Coming from an electronic engineering degree, I have been fortunate to have an all-rounded technological prowess and a knack for applying technology effectively in real-world scenarios. This ability was one of the most highlighted skills coming from my senior peers. The second of which was the ability to work productively with anyone in a team.


My current software forte is full-stack Ruby on Rails development, which is soon to be coupled with JavaScript and React.


My motivation lies in creating value which revolves around my core-beliefs of:

  • Consistent Growth - This starts with being open to learning from any source or person.
  • Embracing Challenges - We can best learn by accepting things as they are and then working from there.
  • Freedom - Giving myself and others the space to be; with respect, tolerance and understanding.

The following are some of my defining traits:

  • Sharing new pieces of information or experiences that I find profound excites me, which have lead to the enjoyment of teaching/facilitating classes as well as writing about my learnings.
  • I connect best with others over an activity, whether that is a road-trip or braai-ing (barbeques); in any space that allows for one-on-one conversations.
  • Creating space in my day is essential as it creates an opportunity to reflect, explore new ideas and improve all areas of my life.
  • I have a strong drive to be independent and much of my motivation to work today comes from the idea that I am creating long term value, of which the returns are space, freedom and choice.
  • I love multi-day hikes; my yearly adventure is always the highlight (this is where the hero-image above stems from).

If you believe I will be a great fit for your team (and project) please contact me.
